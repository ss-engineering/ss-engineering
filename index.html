About Us

S S ENGINEERING is a full-service fabrication and mechanical solutions company based in Faridabad. We specialize in precision fabrication, CNC lathe machining, press work, galvanization, and zinc plating. Our expertise combines craftsmanship and modern technology to deliver reliable, high-quality engineering outcomes. With a focus on customization and durability, we proudly serve automotive, industrial, and custom fabrication clients.

Our Services

  • Fabrication: Sheet metal, structures, and precision assemblies.
  • Press Shop: Advanced mechanical press operations up to 150mm bolts.
  • CNC Machining: High-precision lathe and milling for custom parts.
  • Galvanisation & Zinc Plating: Long-lasting anti-corrosion finishes.
  • Custom Paint Jobs: Durable industrial paint solutions with aesthetic appeal.
